HOW TO PLAY

The Meta-Grid: 27 positions arranged as a 3×3×3 cube. Each position holds a local 3×3×3 tic-tac-toe game (3 layers of 3×3 = 27 cells each).
Local game: Each local game is itself 3D — 3 layers stacked. Get 3 in a row in any row, column, pillar, or diagonal within that local cube to claim it.
Routing: Your local cell position sends your opponent to the matching meta-position. If that meta-position is already claimed, they play anywhere free.
Win condition: Claim 3 meta-positions in a 3D line (row, column, pillar, or space diagonal) to WIN THE CUBE.
Views: Layered = 3 meta-layers stacked. Flat = all 27 in a 9×3 grid. Navigator = click a meta-position to zoom in.
